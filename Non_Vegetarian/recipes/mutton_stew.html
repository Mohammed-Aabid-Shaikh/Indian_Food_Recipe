Mutton Stew

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 1 hour Total Time: 1 hour 20 mins

Mutton Stew is a hearty and comforting dish, perfect for cool evenings. It's made with tender mutton, fresh vegetables, and aromatic spices for a soul-warming experience!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams mutton (cut into pieces)
  • 2 medium onions (sliced)
  • 2 medium carrots (chopped)
  • 2 potatoes (diced)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • Whole spices (cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pan. Add whole spices and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add sliced onions and cook until translucent.
  3. Mix in ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add mutton pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Add turmeric, black pepper powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add carrots, potatoes, and enough water to cover the mutton. Bring to a boil.
  7. Lower the heat, cover, and simmer until the mutton is tender (about 45 minutes).
  8. Pour in coconut milk and garam masala. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice, appam, or crusty bread.
