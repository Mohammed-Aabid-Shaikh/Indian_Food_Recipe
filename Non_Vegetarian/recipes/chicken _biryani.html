Chicken Biryani

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 40 mins Total Time: 1 hour

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic Indian dish made with tender chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of spices. Perfect for a festive meal!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot, add whole spices and sauté until aromatic.
  2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Mix in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add chicken, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until chicken is partially done.
  5. Stir in tomatoes and yogurt. Cook until the chicken is tender and the masala thickens.
  6. Add soaked rice and water, mix well.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked.
  8. Garnish with coriander and mint leaves. Serve hot with raita.
