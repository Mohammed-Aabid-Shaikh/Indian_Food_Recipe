Murgh Dum Biryani

Prep Time: 40 mins Cook Time: 1 hr Total Time: 1 hr 40 mins

Murgh Dum Biryani is a classic Indian dish made with layers of fragrant basmati rice, tender chicken, and aromatic spices, all cooked together on a slow flame for a rich and flavorful experience.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams chicken (bone-in or boneless)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 large onions (sliced and fried)
  • 3 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 2-3 saffron strands (soaked in warm milk)
  • 3 cups water (for rice)
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaf)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Boil the basmati rice with whole spices and salt until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pot, add marinated chicken, and cook until partially done.
  4. Layer the chicken with cooked rice, fried onions, coriander, and mint leaves.
  5. Drizzle saffron milk and additional ghee on top of the rice layer.
  6. Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on a low flame (dum) for 30-40 minutes.
  7. Mix gently before serving. Enjoy hot with raita or salad.
