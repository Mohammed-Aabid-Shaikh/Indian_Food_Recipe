Fish Makhani

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Total Time: 50 mins

Fish Makhani is a creamy, flavorful dish made with tender fish fillets in a rich tomato and butter-based gravy. This dish is perfect for special occasions and pairs beautifully with naan or basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams fish fillets (boneless)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 3 medium tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate the fish with yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and lightly fry the fish fillets until golden on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter and add the tomato puree. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Slowly stir in the cream and cook on low heat for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the fried fish and kasuri methi. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or steamed rice.
