Mutton Curry

Prep Time: 25 mins Cook Time: 1 hour Total Time: 1 hour 25 mins

Mutton Curry is a rich and spicy Indian dish made with tender pieces of mutton slow-cooked in a flavorful blend of spices. A perfect choice for a hearty meal!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams mutton (cut into pieces)
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 3 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pressure cooker, add whole spices and sauté until aromatic.
  2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Mix in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add mutton, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until mutton is browned.
  5. Stir in tomato puree and yogurt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  6. Add water, close the lid, and pressure cook for 6-7 whistles or until mutton is tender.
  7. Open the lid, sprinkle garam masala, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
