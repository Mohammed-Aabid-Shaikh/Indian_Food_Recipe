Kheer

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Total Time: 40 mins

Kheer is a traditional Indian rice pudding made with milk, rice, sugar, and flavored with cardamom, saffron, and dry fruits. It's a comforting dessert enjoyed on festive occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup basmati rice
  • 4 cups full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron strands (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cashews
  • 2 tablespoons chopped almonds
  • 2 tablespoons golden raisins
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 15 minutes.
  2. In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat ghee and sauté the cashews, almonds, and raisins until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the soaked rice and sauté for 2-3 minutes on medium heat.
  4. Pour in the milk and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is soft and the milk thickens.
  5. Add sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron (if using), and cook for another 5 minutes until the sugar dissolves completely.
  6. Garnish with the fried dry fruits and serve warm or chilled.
