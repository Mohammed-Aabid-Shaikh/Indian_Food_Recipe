Kalakand

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 40 mins Total Time: 50 mins

Kalakand is a rich and creamy Indian sweet made with paneer and condensed milk. Its crumbly texture and delicious taste make it a perfect treat for festivals and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams fresh paneer (or ricotta cheese)
  • 1 can (400 grams) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (optional, adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped pistachios and almonds (for garnish)
  • A few strands of saffron (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crumble the paneer into a non-stick pan or heavy-bottomed skillet.
  2. Add the condensed milk and mix well.
  3. Cook on medium heat, stirring continuously to avoid sticking.
  4. As the mixture thickens, add sugar (if needed), cardamom powder, and saffron strands.
  5. Keep cooking until the mixture leaves the sides of the pan and forms a dough-like consistency.
  6. Transfer the mixture to a greased tray or plate and flatten it evenly.
  7. Garnish with chopped pistachios and almonds. Press them gently into the mixture.
  8. Allow it to cool completely. Cut into squares or diamond shapes before serving.
