Jalebi

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Total Time: 40 mins

Jalebi is a classic Indian dessert, known for its crispy and sugary spirals. Perfect for celebrations, this sweet treat is irresistible when served warm or cold.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water (for syrup)
  • A pinch of saffron strands
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Ghee or oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, cornstarch, and yogurt. Gradually add water to form a smooth, pancake-like batter. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the sugar syrup by boiling 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup water until it reaches one-string consistency. Add saffron strands and cardamom powder. Set aside.
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  4. Fill a piping bag or squeeze bottle with the batter. Pipe the batter into the hot oil in spiral shapes.
  5. Fry the jalebis until golden and crisp. Remove and immediately immerse in warm sugar syrup for 30 seconds. Remove and place on a wire rack.
  6. Serve warm or cold, garnished with a few saffron strands.
