Sandesh

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Total Time: 35 mins

Sandesh is a popular Bengali dessert made with fresh chhena (paneer) and flavored with cardamom. It's soft, sweet, and perfect for festive occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands (optional)
  • Chopped pistachios for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the milk in a pan. Once it starts boiling, add lemon juice gradually to curdle the milk.
  2. Strain the curdled milk using a muslin cloth to separate the chhena (paneer).
  3. Wash the chhena under cold water to remove the lemony taste, then squeeze out excess water.
  4. Knead the chhena for 5-7 minutes until smooth and soft.
  5. Add powdered sugar and cardamom powder to the chhena, mixing well.
  6. Cook the mixture on low heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring continuously, until it thickens slightly.
  7. Shape the mixture into small discs or any desired shape and let it cool.
  8. Garnish with saffron strands and chopped pistachios. Serve chilled.
