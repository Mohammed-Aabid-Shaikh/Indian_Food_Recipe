Malai Kofta

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 40 mins Total Time: 1 hour

Malai Kofta is a rich and creamy North Indian curry made with soft vegetable dumplings served in a flavorful, aromatic gravy. Perfect for festive occasions or special meals, it pairs beautifully with naan or jeera rice.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 100 grams grated paneer
  • 50 grams boiled and mashed carrots
  • 50 grams boiled peas
  • 3 tablespoons cornflour
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 2 medium onions, pureed
  • 3 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • ½ cup fresh cream
  • 1 teaspoon kasoori methi
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes, paneer, boiled carrots, peas, cornflour, salt, garam masala, and red chili powder. Form small koftas and deep fry until golden brown. Set aside.
  2. Heat butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add onion puree and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, and tomato puree. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  4. Add cream, kasoori methi, and salt. Mix well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the fried koftas just before serving. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
