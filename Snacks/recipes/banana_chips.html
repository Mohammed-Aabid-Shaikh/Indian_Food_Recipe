Banana Chips

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Total Time: 35 mins

Banana chips are a crispy and savory snack made by deep frying thin slices of raw bananas, typically served as a snack in India or enjoyed during festivals.

Ingredients

  • 3 raw bananas
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil (for frying)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

Instructions

  1. Peel the raw bananas and slice them thinly using a mandoline slicer or knife.
  2. Soak the banana slices in water for about 5 minutes to prevent browning.
  3. Drain the water and pat the slices dry using a kitchen towel.
  4. Heat coconut oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Carefully drop the banana slices into the hot oil and fry in batches until golden and crispy.
  6. Remove the chips from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. While the chips are still hot, sprinkle with salt, turmeric, and optional spices (black pepper and chili powder).
  8. Allow the chips to cool before serving. Store in an airtight container.
