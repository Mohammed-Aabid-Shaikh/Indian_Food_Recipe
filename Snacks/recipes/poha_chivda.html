Poha Chivda

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Total Time: 20 mins

Poha Chivda is a light and crispy Indian snack made with flattened rice, peanuts, and flavorful spices. It's perfect as a tea-time companion or a quick snack on the go.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups thin flattened rice (poha)
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • 1/4 cup roasted chana dal
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Dry roast the poha in a pan on low heat until crisp. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in the same pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves, green chilies, and peanuts. Fry until the peanuts are golden brown.
  4. Add roasted chana dal and turmeric powder. Stir well.
  5. Mix in the roasted poha, sugar, and salt. Toss to coat evenly with the spices.
  6. Cool completely and store in an airtight container.
