Chakli

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Total Time: 45 mins

Chakli is a popular Indian crispy snack made from rice flour, chickpea flour, and spices, often enjoyed with tea or as a festive treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1/2 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon ajwain (carom seeds)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon hot oil (for the dough)
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, chickpea flour, sesame seeds, cumin seeds, ajwain, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  2. Add hot oil and mix well.
  3. Gradually add water to form a smooth, stiff dough.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Fill a chakli maker or piping bag with the dough.
  6. Press the dough into spiral shapes in the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Drain the fried chaklis on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Allow them to cool completely before serving or storing in an airtight container.
