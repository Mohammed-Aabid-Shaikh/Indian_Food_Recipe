Samosa

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Total Time: 50 mins

Samosa is a popular Indian snack consisting of a crispy pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas. It's a favorite treat at tea-time or festive gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 large potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, oil, and water until a firm dough forms. Cover and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once aromatic, add boiled potatoes and peas.
  3. Add turmeric powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and let the filling cool.
  4. Divide the dough into small portions. Roll out each portion into a circle, cut into halves, and shape into cones.
  5. Fill the cones with the potato mixture, seal the edges with water, and form triangles.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the samosas until golden brown. Serve hot with chutney.
