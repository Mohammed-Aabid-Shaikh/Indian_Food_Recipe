Khaman

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Total Time: 30 mins

Khaman is a popular Indian snack, typically enjoyed as a breakfast or a tea-time snack. Made from steamed gram flour, it is soft, spongy, and topped with mustard seeds and coriander leaves.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon green chili paste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for batter)
  • 2 tablespoons oil (for tempering)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix besan, turmeric, ginger paste, green chili paste, yogurt, sugar, salt, and water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Add baking soda to the batter and mix well.
  3. Grease a steaming tray or a shallow pan, pour the batter into it, and steam it for 15-20 minutes until fully cooked.
  4. Meanwhile, heat oil in a pan for tempering. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and slit green chili, and let them splutter.
  5. Once the khaman is cooked, cut it into small cubes and pour the tempering over it.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
