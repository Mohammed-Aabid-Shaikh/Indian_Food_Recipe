Shakarpara

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Total Time: 35 mins

Shakarpara is a delightful Indian snack made with wheat flour, sugar, and ghee. These crispy, sweet bites are perfect for festivals and tea-time indulgence.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, powdered sugar, and cardamom powder.
  2. Add ghee and combine until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. Gradually add water to form a firm dough. Cover and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough to 1/4-inch thickness and cut into diamond-shaped pieces.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the pieces on medium heat until golden brown.
  6. Let the Shakarpara cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
